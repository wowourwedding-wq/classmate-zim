Common questions

Frequently asked.

Does it need internet to work?

No. After the first visit, ClassMate Zim works completely offline — you can plan lessons, mark attendance, record marks and run the whole planner from a kombi, a power cut, or anywhere with no signal. Install it once, use it anywhere.

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your phone or laptop. We never receive your class lists, marks, parent numbers or anything else. There's no server, no account, no login. Export a backup file in Settings to save a copy.

What if my phone is lost or stolen?

Export a backup file (Settings → Export backup) regularly — once a week is enough — and email it to yourself. On a new phone, open the planner, then Settings → Import backup, and everything is back.

Can I really send bulk messages to parents?

Yes — two ways. WhatsApp: we generate one personalised link per parent; you tap each, WhatsApp opens with the message ready, you hit send. SMS bulk: we copy the phone numbers + messages to your clipboard; paste into BulkSMS.co.zw, EcoCash bulk, Africa's Talking — any gateway you already use.

Does it support Cambridge syllabus, not just ZIMSEC?

Yes. The subject library covers both — ECD through A-Level for ZIMSEC, and Cambridge Primary, Lower Secondary, IGCSE and AS / A-Level for Cambridge. Switch in Profile or use "Both" if your school runs both syllabi.

How do schools get custom branding?

School Edition pre-loads your school's logo and colours so every teacher's planner looks like your school's official planner. Buy School Edition, send us your logo + colours, and we configure a branded build for you. Annual updates included.

How do I pay?

EcoCash, Mukuru, InnBucks, USD cash, or PayPal. Tap the order button — it opens a WhatsApp chat with us, we send payment details, you confirm, we send you your activation. Schools get an invoice for finance.

Can I use it on both my phone and my laptop?

Yes — install it on each device. Use Settings → Export backup on one, and Import backup on the other to sync up. The data lives on each device individually, so you decide when to sync.

Use it like an app

Install ClassMate Zim on your phone in 3 taps.

No App Store. No Play Store. No download size. Just open the planner in your browser, install it to your home screen, and it behaves like a native app — even with no internet.

1

Open the planner

Tap Open the planner at the top of this page (or visit classmate-zim.netlify.app/app). It loads instantly, no waiting.

2

Tap your browser menu

Android (Chrome / Edge): tap the ⋮ menu and choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
iPhone (Safari): tap the Share button, scroll down, and tap Add to Home Screen.

3

Open from your home screen

An app icon appears on your phone like any other app. Tap it — ClassMate opens full-screen, with no browser bars, and works offline forever.
